Setting it up


Step 1

Choose the items where you want to hide your clues. The order in which you select your areas will determine the treasure hunt path. The last spot won't have a clue as you'll be putting the your treasure there!

Step 2

Select which theme you'd like. At the moment we only have pirates but look out in the future for more themes like medieval or fantasy.

Step 3

Select the difficulty. Are your hunters as sharp as they seem? Don't make it too difficult or they'll never get their treasure.
