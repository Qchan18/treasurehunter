Select Available Items

Click on the items in the order of your treasure hunt. This will determine where your hunter/s will be going. You can make it as long as you want or as short as you want. You can check the clue by clicking on the name of the item.

The first item will always be: Phone Charger

The final item is where you should put your treasure.
