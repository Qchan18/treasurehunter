Printing your Treasure Hunt

Tips on the best way to print

Follow the steps below for the best results.

Step 1. Print out the generated pdf puzzle file.

Step 2. Once printed apply 3M Post-it Notes (36 x 48mm/1 3/8 in x 1 7/8in) onto the printed QR codes.

Step 3. Re-print the generated pdf file and watch as the QR codes get printed onto the Post-it notes.

Step 4. Cut the Post-it notes in half and being placing them around the house.

Step 5. Remeber to send the starting page to your hunter or they won't be able to start!
